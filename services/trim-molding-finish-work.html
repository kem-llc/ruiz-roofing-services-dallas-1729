Trim, Molding & Finish Work

Elegant crown molding, baseboards, wainscoting, and more to add character.

Schedule this service

Overview

The details make the difference. Our finish carpentry services include crown molding, chair rails, wainscoting, window and door casings, and custom mantels. We carefully measure, cut, and install each piece to create seamless transitions and polished interiors.

What's included

  • Enhances home value and aesthetics
  • Precise miters and coped joints
  • Wide selection of profiles and woods
  • Painting or staining included

How it works

1

Design & Material Selection

We help you choose molding profiles and wood species that match your home's style.

2

Measurement & Layout

We measure each room and plan the layout for optimal visual flow.

3

Installation & Touch-Up

Our carpenters install the trim, fill nail holes, and sand for a flawless finish.
