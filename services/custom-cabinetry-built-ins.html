Custom Cabinetry & Built-Ins

Beautiful, functional cabinets and shelving designed to fit your space perfectly.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our custom cabinetry transforms kitchens, bathrooms, and living areas. We design and build each piece to your exact specifications, choosing from a wide range of woods, finishes, and hardware. From modern minimalist to traditional, we bring your style to life.

What's included

  • Fully customizable dimensions and layout
  • Solid wood construction with dovetail joints
  • Soft-close doors and drawers available
  • Maximizes storage and organization

How it works

1

Design Consultation

We measure your space, discuss your needs, and create a 3D design for your approval.

2

Material & Finish Selection

Choose from oak, maple, cherry, or custom finishes. We also help with hardware selection.

3

Fabrication & Installation

Cabinets are built in our shop, then installed with precision. We ensure everything is level and aligned.
