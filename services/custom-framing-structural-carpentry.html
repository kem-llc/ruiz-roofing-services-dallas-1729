Custom Framing & Structural Carpentry

Precision framing for walls, roofs, and additions that meet code and stand strong.

Overview

Our framing team brings decades of experience to new construction and remodels. We handle everything from load-bearing walls to roof trusses, ensuring your structure is square, level, and built to last. We work closely with engineers and architects to execute plans accurately.

What's included

  • Meets all Dallas building codes
  • Uses kiln-dried, graded lumber
  • Reduces waste with precise measurements
  • Seamless coordination with other trades

How it works

1

Consultation & Planning

We review blueprints or discuss your vision, take measurements, and identify material needs.

2

Material Selection & Prep

We source high-quality lumber and prep the site for efficient work.

3

Framing & Inspection

Common questions

Do you work with homeowners for small framing projects?

Absolutely. Whether it's a new interior wall or a garage expansion, we handle projects of all sizes.

How long does framing typically take?

Timeline depends on scope. A simple wall can be done in a day; a full addition may take several weeks. We'll provide a schedule upfront.
