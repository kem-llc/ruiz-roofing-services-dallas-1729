Deck & Porch Construction

Outdoor living spaces built with durable materials and expert craftsmanship.

Schedule this service

Overview

Enhance your home's outdoor living with a custom deck or porch. We design and build structures that complement your architecture and landscape. Using pressure-treated lumber, cedar, or composite materials, we create spaces for relaxation and entertainment that withstand Texas weather.

What's included

  • Custom design to fit your yard and lifestyle
  • Durable, weather-resistant materials
  • Built-in seating, planters, and lighting options
  • Includes railings, stairs, and lattice

How it works

1

Site Assessment & Design

We evaluate your yard, discuss your vision, and design a deck that meets your needs.

2

Material Selection

Choose from wood or composite decking. We explain the pros and cons of each.

3

Construction & Finishing

Our crew builds the deck, applies sealant or stain, and cleans up thoroughly.
