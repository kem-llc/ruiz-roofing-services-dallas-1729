Our story

Dallas's Trusted Carpentry Experts

To deliver exceptional craftsmanship that enhances homes and lives, building lasting relationships through quality, integrity, and personalized service.

Ruiz Roofing Services started as a small family operation in 2005, founded by master carpenter Carlos Ruiz. Growing up in Dallas, Carlos learned the trade from his father, a skilled woodworker who believed that every piece of wood had potential. After years of honing his skills on residential and commercial projects, Carlos decided to start his own company with a simple mission: provide honest, high-quality carpentry at fair prices.

Over the years, Ruiz Roofing has grown into a trusted name in Dallas, known for meticulous attention to detail and reliable service. We've built everything from custom libraries to backyard decks, always treating each project as if it were our own home. Today, our team continues that tradition, combining old-world craftsmanship with modern techniques to create spaces that our clients love.
